Retrospective

What's a retrospective?

A time to reflect on how we are doing and to find ways to improve. We'll talk about things to:

  • Start doing
  • Stop doing
  • Continue doing

Write on sticky notes things that are going well, things that are holding you back, and things that are just ok but could improve.
